Some services we offer

Vintage Indian Motorcycle Restorations

We handle any job from servicing, carburettor, speedometer, generator, distributor overhauls, wheel building, transmission, full engine rebuilds to complete restorations on vintage Indian Motorcycles.

Frame and Fork Straightening

This is our personal specialty with a comprehensive frame jig, designed and built under the supervision of leading motorcycle engineer Lindsay Urquhart, is in place on the premises and is supported by factory frame drawings and a full range of available reproduction frame castings.

Motorcycles and Parts For Sale

We always have a range of machines on display for sale, including Fours, Chiefs,Scouts, Despatch Tows and Traffic Cars. Over the counter and mail order supply of a large range of parts from leading US and Australian manufacturers are also available for most models.
